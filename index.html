Our Story

Founded in 2018, Semilla Coffee Lab was born from our passion for quality coffee and creative community spaces. We believe great ideas emerge over perfectly brewed cups.

Our café doubles as a co-working space, offering free high-speed WiFi, power outlets at every table, and a quiet atmosphere for focus.
